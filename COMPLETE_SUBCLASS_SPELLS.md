## TOTAL UNIQUE SPELLS TO TAG

Counting all unique spell names across all subclasses: ~120-150 spells
Many spells appear in multiple subclasses (e.g., "Freedom of Movement" appears 5 times)

## FORMAT FOR TAGGING

Each spell needs to add all applicable subclass tags:

```typescript
subclasses: ["forge domain cleric", "light domain cleric"]  // if spell appears in multiple
```

Use format: `"{subclass name} {class}"` (all lowercase)
For druids: `"circle of the land (terrain) druid"`
